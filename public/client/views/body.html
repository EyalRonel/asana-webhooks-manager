Asana Webhooks Manager

Asana webhooks manager (AWM) is an open source, straight forward, management tool and event handler for Asana's webhooks API.
It provides a complete solution to manage webhooks subscription and accept event payloads from Asana in real-time.

Consider AWM as your starting point for writing your own application logic, on top of Asana's webhook notifications mechanism.
